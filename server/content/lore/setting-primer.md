# Setting primer

> Placeholder copy pending Angelus's approved text.

Terra Mortis is a *Vampire: The Requiem* 2nd Edition chronicle. This primer will
orient a new player to the city, its factions, and the tone of play. The text
below is placeholder scaffolding only: it exists to exercise the renderer, not to
describe the real setting, which Angelus will supply or approve later.

## The city

The chronicle is set in a version of the city rewritten for the Danse Macabre.
By night the streets belong to the Kindred, and every favour is remembered. The
real primer will name the districts and the mood of each.

## The covenants at a glance

The five covenants each hold a different answer to the same question of how the
dead should live among the living:

- The **Invictus**, who prize order and Honour.
- The **Carthian Movement**, who argue for something newer.
- The **Lancea et Sanctum**, who carry an older faith.
- The **Ordo Dracul**, who study what the Blood can become.
- The **Circle of the Crone**, who keep the older rites.

## How to read this site

1. Start with this primer for the mood and the map.
2. Read the **[game guide](game-guide)** for how a night of play runs.
3. Keep the **[rules](rules)** and **[friendly errata](friendly-errata)** to hand

A single house maxim sets the register:

> The Blood keeps its own counsel, and so should you.

Mechanical terms appear inline as code, for example a `Resolve + Composure`
reflexive roll, and longer worked notes appear as a block:

```
Pool  = Attribute + Skill (+ Specialisation, +1 die)
Rote  = roll twice, keep the better result (Terra Mortis house rule)
```
