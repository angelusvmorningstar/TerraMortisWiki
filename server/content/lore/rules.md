## Dice pools

Terra Mortis uses the Storytelling System. A pool is built from an Attribute plus
a Skill, and every 8, 9, or 10 is a success. The house rote rule is *roll twice,
keep the better result*.

```
Successes = count of dice showing 8, 9, or 10
10-again  = a 10 is re-rolled and may add more successes
Rote      = the whole pool is rolled twice, better result kept
```

## Common actions

- **Feeding** is declared in your downtime and validated, then rolled live before
  the next session. It is never invented for you.
- **Investigations** clear a threshold of successes over one or more actions.
- **Merit actions** are judged on their content by the Storyteller and are *not*
  rolled unless a rule says so.

## Order of resolution

The Storyteller resolves a downtime in a fixed order so the numbers stay honest:

1. Blood Sorcery rites first.
2. Feeding pools next.
3. Project actions after that.
4. Merit and influence actions last.
